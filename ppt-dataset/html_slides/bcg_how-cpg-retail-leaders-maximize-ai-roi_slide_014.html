Retail Demand Value Chain
🛍 Retail
AI layerDescription
Offer to AssortmentIdentify shopper needs, preferences, and emerging local patterns. Optimise entry-to-market innovation and promotion efficiency with real-time margin insights. Support shopper intent pre-visit and experience in-store to drive conversion
