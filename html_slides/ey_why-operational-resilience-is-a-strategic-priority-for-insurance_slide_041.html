In terms of the most important skill sets for the next five years, CROs see several different types of talent as comparably valuable. Diverse and holistic skills are necessary for effective risk management on both the first and second lines.

On the first line, the priorities are cybersecurity (36%), business/product knowledge (27%), change and transformation (25%), operational risk and business continuity (25%) and ML/AI (23%).
