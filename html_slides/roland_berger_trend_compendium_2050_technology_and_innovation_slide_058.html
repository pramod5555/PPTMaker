> Considering specific occupations supported by latest projections, shifting dynamics in the jobs market become apparent: Increasingly redundant roles will decline from 15.4% of the workforce in 2020 to 9% in 2025. Emerging occupations will grow from 7.8% to 13.5% of the total employee base

> However, investigating 26 advanced economies, there will be around 12 million more jobs available: 85 million jobs will be displaced by a shift in the division of labor between humans and machines, while 97 million jobs will be created by new divisions of labor between humans, machines and algorithms
