Chapter 8
Our survey results show clear maturation of controls
environments on both the first and second lines, though
ample work remains to be done. The most mature aspects
of the control framework are governance and oversight,
risk assessment, and risk control and identification.
Testing and monitoring are the least mature aspects.
There is much more room for maturation when it comes
to first-line adoption of specific components of the
internal control framework, with no more than 30% of
respondents saying that any aspect of their control
environment is mature.
