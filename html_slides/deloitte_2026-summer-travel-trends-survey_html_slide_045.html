Deloitte Consumer Industry Center
The Deloitte Consumer Industry Center provides premiere insights based on primary research on the most prevalent issues facing the consumer industry to
help our clients run effectively and achieve superior business results. The center is your trusted source for information on leading trends and research that
connect insights, issues, and solutions for Deloitte's four consumer sectors: automotive; consumer products; retail, wholesale, and distribution; and
transportation, hospitality, and services.
