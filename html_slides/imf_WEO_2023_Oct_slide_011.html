ASSUMPTIONS AND CONVENTIONS

In the tables and figures, the following conventions apply:

  • Tables and figures that list their source as "IMF staff calculations" or "IMF staff estimates" draw on data from the WEO database.
  • When countries are not listed alphabetically, they are ordered on the basis of economic size.
  • Minor discrepancies between sums of constituent figures and totals shown reflect rounding.
  • Composite data are provided for country groups organized according to economic characteristics or region.
  • The boundaries, colors, denominations, and other information shown on maps do not imply any IMF judgment on legal status.

As used in this report, the terms "country" and "economy" do not always refer to a territorial entity that is a state as understood by international law and practice.
