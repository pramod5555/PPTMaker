Disclaimer: The Global Financial Stability Report is a survey by the IMF staff published twice a year, in the spring and fall. The report draws on the financial surveillance of economic issues highlighted in the IMF's World Economic Outlook. The report was prepared by IMF staff and has benefited from comments and suggestions from Executive Directors following their discussion of the report on October 2024. The views expressed in this publication are those of the IMF staff and do not necessarily represent the views of the IMF's Executive Directors or their national authorities.
