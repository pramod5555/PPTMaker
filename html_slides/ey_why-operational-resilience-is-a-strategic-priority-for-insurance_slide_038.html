In terms of reporting lines, responsibilities and
interactions with different functions, our results
show the expanding remit of CROs and their
increased involvement across the business and with
different levels of the organization. A large majority
(72%) of CROs have a direct relationship with the
board or a board-level risk committee and nearly as
many (65%) report directly to the CEO, with about a
quarter (27%) reporting to the CFO.
