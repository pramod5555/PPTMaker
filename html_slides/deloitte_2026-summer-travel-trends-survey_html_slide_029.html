Most travelers are not looking for the ultimate travel
research shortcut. Only 1 in 10 say they find travel
research frustrating, and 26% say they want a one-stop
shop. On the other hand, 57% say they enjoy researching
options on various travel sites.
Even as large platforms lead, users still engage with an
average of 2.5 different types of gen AI travel tools,
suggesting that discovery, comparison, and variety remain
central to the travel planning experience.
