CHAPTER 1
Cyber and third-party risk are CROs’ top priorities
for the next 12 months. Compliance risks appear
to be a more urgent priority for Europe, the
Middle East, India and Africa (EMEIA) (which
may be a function of the higher volume of rules
and requirements there), though they are still
important overall to CROs everywhere. Equity risk,
which was cited by only 5% of survey respondents,
would outside of the top 20 near-term risks, would
almost certainly have been higher had our
research been conducted deeper into 2025. CROs
of European-based companies may also feel some
sense of relief relative to third-party risk since
the implementation of the Digital Operational
Resilience Act (DORA) in January 2025.
