Why this
matters now
People are seeking out enriching experiences in the physical world, creating opportunities for brands to create more vibrant, textural experiences that connect with customers’ lives offline. We don’t expect people to abandon digital technology, but we do believe more people will seek to adjust the balance, scaling their use of screens back in favor of real-world experiences.
This movement is happening now, and we expect to see it grow both in scope and popularity, so brands that get on board with it now will likely set themselves apart.
