> Applying biased or discriminatory
   AI to the real world, enforces a
   vicious cycle – as was the case
   of Amazon and their use of AI to
   review job applicants' CVs in
   order to automate the search for
   talent; the AI deployed turned out

   been trained to vet applications

   As most CVs were submitted by
