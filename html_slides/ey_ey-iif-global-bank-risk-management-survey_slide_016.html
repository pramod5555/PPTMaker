To manage the risks associated with
private credit, CROs are looking to adjust
their exposure limits to nonbank financial
institutions, more closely examine
counterparty and concentration risk, and
enhance scenario planning. G-SIB CROs
are much more likely to be pursuing these
enhancements, befitting their much
greater exposures.
