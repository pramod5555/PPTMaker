Global patents in selected industries, 1980-2019

technologies related to
Industry 4.0 and digitalization
have experienced an
explosion since the mid
1990s, regarding research
and patents

> Patents closely related to
Industry 4.0 have seen a
tenfold increase from 36,000
in 1980 to 385,000 in 2019

> Digitalization has also
initiated a patent push in
related fields such as
medical and environmental
technology and transport

> Technologies that are related
