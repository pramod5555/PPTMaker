INSURANCE RISK MANAGEMENT SURVEY
The fact that climate risk mechanisms are embedded is clear evidence of risk management’s maturation in this area. CROs in Asia-Pacific are also much more likely to say that they conduct scenario analysis and/or stress testing (92% of Asia-Pacific CROs versus 64% of all respondents), have policies in place for businesses impacted by climate change (54% of Asia-Pacific CROs versus 36% of overall), and quantitatively assess the potential impact of transition risks of moving to a lower carbon economy where relevant (46% versus 33% overall).

Interestingly, the results of climate scenario analyses are used for internal, informational purposes only (56%) or inform regulatory requirements (49%). But only 20% of CROs say the results inform corporate strategy and only 16% say they inform the setting of risk appetite.
