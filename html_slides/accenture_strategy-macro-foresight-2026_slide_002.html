About this document
The monthly brief is intended to inform executive teams,
boards and investors on the state of the economy. It has been
prepared based on data as of December 10, 2025.

Each brief includes a summary of global business-relevant
macroeconomic developments, and a set of indicators that
track the overall health of the economy, business activity and
consumers.
