Chapter 3

A large majority (75%) of CROs say risk's involvement with major change initiatives is increasing and virtually no survey respondents report a reduction in risk's involvement. That's very good news, confirming our theory from last year about CROs' expanding strategic role. As one CRO told us, "We think about every piece of transformation and innovation as a partnership opportunity between the business and risk." Another commented: “If you aren’t an active participant, that’s a problem. This should be a requirement of every CRO in the future."

Business-first perspectives such as these will continue to drive the evolution of the CRO role, though they are not the rule at every insurer. However, raising independent challenges when appropriate remains a critical part of CROs' jobs, which may be more challenging if they are involved in formulating strategies.
