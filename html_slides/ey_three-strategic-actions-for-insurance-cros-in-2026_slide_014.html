CHAPTER
Technology
and risk
transformation:
from foundations
to AI at scale
Risk management technology priorities
over the next three to five years are
increasingly defined by a heightened
conviction and quickening pace of
execution. Over the past three years,
CROs’ technology roadmaps have remained
focused on a consistent set of investments:
enterprise governance, risk and compliance
(GRC) platforms, automated controls
testing and AI-enabled risk solutions.
