  • Match tenor and structure of private-credit facilities to underlying asset cash flows (e.g., avoid short-dated bullet maturities on long-dated projects)
  • Monitor build up of private credit facilities alongside traditional bank lines to avoid hidden leverage
  • Tighten working-capital management i.e.,.
    • Faster billing and collections
    • Smarter inventory and procurement to free up cash
  • Protect and grow cash-margin, not just revenue:
    • Prioritise high-margin products, contracts and sites
    • Pause or reshape low-return initiatives
  • Prepare cost-flexibility playbooks (e.g., pre-agreed levers for slowing hiring, reducing discretionary spend, and resizing capacity if volumes disappoint)
  • Improve data, forecasting, and dashboards to cover:
    • Short, frequent cash, and covenant forecasts
    • Shared view between finance and operations on headroom and risks
