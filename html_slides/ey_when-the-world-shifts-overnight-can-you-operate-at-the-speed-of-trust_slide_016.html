Summary
Traditional, slow and periodic risk operating models are no longer fit for a NAVI risk environment.
To operate at the “speed of trust,” organizations must redesign risk to be strategy-first, trigger-based, and governance-forward. This involves anchoring risk in future strategic assumptions, using predefined signals and thresholds to activate action, and aligning governance to move at speed. To succeed, transformation initiatives will tailor their approach to their company’s specific constraints and circumstances.
